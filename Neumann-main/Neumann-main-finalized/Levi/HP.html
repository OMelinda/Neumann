    About our school

    Students who pass the central written entrance exam and are interested in IT or one of its branches are admitted to the 9th grade. In the preparatory language section, we teach the English language and computer science in the first academic year in a special number of hours, followed by 5 years of computer science sector education. At the end of the 13th year, the training concludes with a technician's diploma and graduation certificate. We prepare our students for intermediate or advanced-level graduation in Hungarian, mathematics, history, as well as English or German. If required, physics, physical education, etc. can also be chosen as a graduation subject. Among our classrooms, 14 computer rooms with Internet access, Wi-Fi, a gymnasium, a sports court, a fitness room, a specialist classroom, a library, a student club, a kitchen, a dining room, and a buffet support education and make the school feel like home.
